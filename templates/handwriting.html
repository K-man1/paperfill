Make it your handwriting Pro

Turn your own handwriting into a font, right here — no apps, no sign-ups. Once built, it shows up as a style when you fill a worksheet.

1 · Print the template

Download the template and print it on plain paper.

Download template (PDF)

2 · Fill it in

Write one character in each cell, sitting on the lines. Use a dark pen. Then take a clear, flat photo or scan of the whole page — keep all four corner squares in frame.

3 · Build your font

Upload your photo and give your handwriting a name.

✓ Your font is ready
